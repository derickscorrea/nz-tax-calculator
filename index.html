Income Tax Refund Calculator

Estimate your NZ tax result for the year ended 31 March 2026 — fully automatic, no IRD website needed.

📋 Step-by-step guide

⌃
1

Log in to myIR

Go to myir.ird.govt.nz ↗ and sign in to your IRD account.

2

Open Income Summary

On the home screen click Income Summary, then select Last income tax year. You'll see a "Summary by type" table.

3

Fill in the Income section below

For each income type, enter the Amount and Deductions / Imputation credits columns exactly as shown in myIR.
⚠️ For salary/wages: use the Amount column for Gross Income — not the Deductions column.

4

Your PIR is suggested automatically

Your Prescribed Investor Rate (PIR) is estimated from your total income. You can override it at any time — find your exact PIR in myIR under My profile → Tax rates.

5

Your result appears instantly

The official 2025–26 NZ tax brackets are built in. Click Tax on taxable income to see the full bracket breakdown.
🟢 Refund — IRD owes you money  ·  🔴 Tax to pay — small balance owed (amounts under ~$50 are usually written off by IRD).
