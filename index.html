📋 Step-by-step guide

⌃
1

Log in to myIR

Go to myir.ird.govt.nz ↗, click Income Summary → Last income tax year. You'll see a "Summary by type" table.

2

Fill in the Income section

Enter each row's Amount and Deductions / Imputation credits as shown in myIR. ⚠️ For salary/wages, use the Amount column for Gross Income — not the Deductions column.

3

Add credits & deductions

Enter any charitable donations to approved NZ charities (you'll get a 33.33% tax credit). Add Working for Families credits if applicable.

4

Check your PIR

Your Prescribed Investor Rate is suggested automatically from your income. Verify yours in myIR under My profile → Tax rates and override if needed.

5

Read your result

Tax brackets are built in — no IRD calculator needed. Click Tax on taxable income to see the full bracket breakdown.
